Quality Control

Quality Is More Than Appearance

For bicycle hubs, quality depends on structure accuracy, bearing fit, rotation smoothness, surface finish, assembly consistency and compatibility with the final wheel system.

1

Incoming Check

Material, components and customer-specific requirements should be reviewed before production.

2

Process Check

Machining dimensions, surface finish, spoke holes, bearing fit and axle compatibility require attention.

3

Final Check

Appearance, logo, color, rotation, assembly and packaging should be checked before delivery.

Common Hub Quality Control Points

Inspection details can be adjusted based on customer drawings, samples and confirmed standards.

Inspection AreaCommon Checking Points
ComponentsMaterial, bearing, axle, end cap, freehub body and related parts.
MachiningDimensions, spoke holes, flange, thread, axle fit and structural consistency.
AssemblyBearing installation, rotation smoothness, end cap fit and functional checking.
AppearanceSurface finish, color consistency, logo position and visible defects.
PackagingLabel, packing method, private label requirements and shipment protection.
