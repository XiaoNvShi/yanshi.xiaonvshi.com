Custom Bicycle Hub Manufacturing for B2B Projects

Not every customer needs a standard catalog hub. Many bicycle brands, wheel brands and OEM projects require customized hub solutions based on product positioning, application scenario, frame compatibility, wheel design and brand identity.


ARC supports custom bicycle hub development covering structure discussion, sample development, specification confirmation, color customization, logo marking and production coordination.

Structure Customization

Hub body structure, axle standard, spoke hole count, spoke interface and brake interface.

Brand Customization

Surface treatment, anodized color, laser logo, private label packaging and product identity.

Project Development

Review drawings, samples, reference products and target market requirements before sample production.

Production Support

Coordinate sample confirmation, pilot order, inspection points and batch production requirements.
