A Manufacturing Partner for Professional Bicycle Hub Projects

ARC HUB factory was established in 2013 and focuses on the design, R&D, manufacturing and sales of high-end hubs for bicycles and e-bikes.


The company is located in Taicang, Jiangsu, with factory-based support for OEM/ODM buyers, bicycle brands, wheel brands, distributors and component buyers.
